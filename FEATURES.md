# Invoiceza - Feature List

## üéØ Core Features

### 1. **Professional Invoice Creation**
- Clean, modern interface matching industry standards
- Real-time preview of invoice as you type
- Multiple invoice themes (Classic, Modern, Minimal, Professional)
- Multi-currency support (USD, EUR, GBP, INR, JPY, CAD, AUD, CNY)

### 2. **Business Profile Management**
- Save your business information as default
- Logo upload with preview (max 2MB)
- Complete business details: name, address, email, phone, website, Tax ID/VAT
- Auto-load saved profile for new invoices

### 3. **Client Management**
- Recent clients list with quick selection
- Separate Bill To and Ship To addresses
- Client information saved automatically
- Search through recent clients

### 4. **Smart Line Items**
- Add unlimited line items
- Duplicate line items with one click
- Individual tax settings per item (taxable checkbox)
- Automatic amount calculation
- Quantity, rate, and amount tracking

### 5. **Advanced Calculations**
- Subtotal calculation
- Flexible tax system (customizable tax label and rate)
- Discount (percentage or fixed amount)
- Shipping costs
- Amount paid tracking
- Balance due calculation
- Tax only applies to taxable items

### 6. **Invoice Management**
- Invoice history with search
- Save and load drafts
- Duplicate existing invoices
- Auto-generate invoice numbers
- Invoice status tracking (Draft, Sent, Paid, Overdue, Cancelled)
- Export/Import invoices as JSON

### 7. **Payment Features**
- Multiple payment methods support
- Bank details section
- Payment terms presets (Due on Receipt, Net 7/15/30/60/90)
- Amount paid tracking
- Balance due highlighting

### 8. **PDF Generation**
- High-quality PDF export
- Professional formatting
- Includes all invoice details
- Logo in PDF
- Customizable notes and terms

### 9. **Settings & Customization**
- Comprehensive settings modal with tabs:
  - Business Info
  - Payment Details
  - Preferences
- Default currency selection
- Custom tax labels (Sales Tax, VAT, GST, etc.)
- Default payment terms
- Default terms & conditions

### 10. **User Experience**
- Sticky header for easy navigation
- Toast notifications for actions
- Loading states for PDF generation
- Keyboard shortcuts support
- Responsive design
- Hover effects and transitions
- Form validation

### 11. **Data Persistence**
- LocalStorage for drafts
- Business profile persistence
- Recent clients storage
- Invoice history (last 50 invoices)
- Auto-save functionality

### 12. **Quick Actions**
- New Invoice
- Save Draft
- Load Draft
- Clear Form
- Duplicate Invoice
- Load Sample Data
- Export as JSON
- Import from JSON
